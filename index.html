Polynomial Regression Simulator

About Polynomial Regression Simulator
This simulator takes in 1) a degree of egression, 2) method of calculation, and 3) user-inputted data points to generate a line of best-fit.

The Least Squares method calculates optimal coefficients through mathematical forumulas aimed at reducing the sum of the squares of the offsets (residual part) of the points from the curve.

While Gradient Descent is a machine learning technical where coefficients are initialized randomly, and then fine-tuned to minimumize of the cost function, thus optimizing towards the best-fit line through a series of adjustments.

Instructions:

Step 1: Select the degree of regression (linear, quadratic, or cubic) or type of curve to fit to.

Step 2: Select the method to use for calculating the best-fit line.

Step 3: Click to draw at least 3 dots in the canvas to generate the best fit line. The line will dynamically update as more data points are added.
